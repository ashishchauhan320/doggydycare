About

one woof day care

Under One Woof opened in 2007 as a daycare and boarding alternative for your pet. We feature a 4000 square foot open play area, that is divided in two for big and little dogs. Your pet gets the opportunity to interact with other dogs and employees in this unique setting. This is much different from other facilities that leave your pet in a kennel all day.

We started out as Paws and Claws of Stillwater as an extention of a facility in Hudson WI. In April 2014 a long time employee and groomer Kristi fulfilled a dream by buying the Stillwater facility and changed the name to Under One Woof. There are more changes yet to come. All to enhance and improve the quality of the experience for both you and your pet.

Meet the Founder

Kristi, Owner

Hello! I have been working in the pet industry since 2002. I started as a groomer with a passion for animals and the dream that someday I would own my own dog daycare facility. That Dream came true in April 2014, when I had the opportunity to purchase the business I had already been grooming at for the last 6 years. I look forward to coming out from behind the clippers; seeing familiar faces (furry and otherwise) and meeting the new ones. We have a beautiful 7 year old Daughter, 2 shepherd mixes; Dakota who is 13 and Pudge who is 4 and a new great dane puppy Aramis. I grew up in White Bear Lake but currently reside in WI. I have a Degree in Physical Therapy and am CPR and First Aid trained for both people and pets. In my free time I like to read and coach gymnastics.
